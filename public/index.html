Frequently asked questions

How do I download a video from X (Twitter)?

Open the post on X, tap Share → Copy link, paste the link into the box above, and press Download. Your video saves as an MP4.

Is it free?

Yes — completely free, no account required, and no watermark added to your video.

Does it work on iPhone and Android?

Yes. It runs in any mobile or desktop browser, so there's no app to install. We have step-by-step guides for iPhone and Android.

Where do the downloaded videos go?

On a computer, to your Downloads folder. On Android, to Downloads. On iPhone, tap the share icon after downloading to save the file into Files or Photos.

What quality will the video be?

You get the highest-quality single-file MP4 that the original post provides — usually the same resolution the uploader posted.

Can I download private or age-restricted videos?

No. The tool only works with public posts, and you should only download videos you own or have permission to use.
