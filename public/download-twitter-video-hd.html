What quality will I get?

X stores each video in a few resolutions and serves the best one your connection can handle. The downloader picks the top rung of that ladder. In practice that means:

  • 1080p (full HD) — if the creator uploaded at that resolution.
  • 720p — the most common quality for X videos.
  • Lower — only if the original itself was low-resolution.

The one thing to understand about "HD"

No downloader can make a video sharper than the original. If someone posts a blurry 480p clip, 480p is the ceiling — tools that promise to "upscale to 4K" are either adding fake, mushy detail or simply lying. What you *can* count on here is getting the true maximum: the exact file quality the uploader provided, untouched.

HD download FAQ

What's the highest quality I can get?

Whatever the original poster uploaded — often 720p or 1080p. X doesn't create quality beyond the source.

Does downloading lower the quality?

No. The original MP4 is saved as-is, with no additional compression.

Can I get 4K?

Only if the original was 4K, which is rare on X. Anything claiming to "upscale" to 4K is adding artificial detail, not real resolution.
