Android makes this easy: unlike iPhone, your browser saves files right to the Downloads folder and they appear in your Gallery automatically. No app, no APK — just paste a post link and tap download.

Step by step on Android

  1. Copy the link. In the X app, tap the share icon under the video and choose Copy link.
  2. Paste it above. Open this page in Chrome (or Samsung Internet) and paste the link into the box.
  3. Tap Download, then tap Save video (MP4) when the preview appears.
  4. Find it. The MP4 saves to Downloads. Open your Files app or Gallery to watch or share it.

Where the video is saved

Android stores browser downloads in the Downloads folder. Most phones also surface videos in the Gallery/Photos app under a "Downloads" album within a minute or two. If you don't see it immediately, open the Files app and browse to Downloads directly.

Tips

  • Works in Chrome, Samsung Internet, and Firefox — use whichever you prefer.

Android FAQ

Where do downloaded X videos go?

To your Downloads folder. Find them in the Files app, or in your Gallery under a Downloads album.

Does it work in Chrome and Samsung Internet?

Yes — any modern Android browser works, including Chrome, Samsung Internet, and Firefox.

Do I need to install an app or APK?

No. Everything runs in your browser, so there's no APK to sideload and no permissions to grant.
